Frequently asked questions

What growing teams ask us first.

Will you work with a business that competes with mine? No. We run our own digital businesses, so we never take on anyone who competes with you.

No. We run our own digital businesses on this exact team, TicketsToDo, VoucherCodes.ae, DCMnetwork and more, so we hold a firm line: we do not take on business that competes with us or you. Your work, your data and your edge stay yours.

Will it sound like us, or like AI? Like you. AI drafts the volume, people edit every piece to your voice.

Like you. AI agents draft at volume on the workflow, and a real writer edits every piece for your brand voice, accuracy and tone. You get the speed of AI without the generic, off-brand feel, and nothing goes out unread.

What does it cost? A 30-day trial, by the job or by the hour. Only pay if you’re happy.

There’s no catch, and it isn’t free work. Start with a 30-day trial, up to 50 hours per role. If you’re happy and want to keep going, you pay for the work from the start, by the job or by the hour, whichever works better for you. If it isn’t right, walk away and pay nothing. You only ever pay when you’re happy with the work.

By the job: a set price per piece, like a product description or an article, so you pay for the result and never for our hours or how we split the work between people and AI.
