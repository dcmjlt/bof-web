Where each fits

AI tool

What an AI tool gives you.

  • ✓Software with subscription pricing.
  • ✓You configure it, train it, monitor it.
  • ✓A learning curve before it pays off.
  • ✓A new system to babysit when it breaks.
  • ✓It handles what it was designed for; judgement calls still come back to you.
BOF

What BOF gives you.

  • ✓A team that runs your back office, with AI built into how the work gets done.
  • ✓People for the judgement, AI agents for the repetitive volume.
  • ✓No tool for you to configure, learn or operate.
  • ✓When tasks repeat, we build the systems and we run them.
  • ✓A human accountable for every outcome.

A back-office team of people and AI agents, hired by the job or by the hour. Not an AI tool you have to operate yourself.

Questions

What growing teams ask us first.

Will you work with a business that competes with mine? No. We run our own digital businesses, so we never take on anyone who competes with you.

No. We run our own digital businesses on this exact team, TicketsToDo, VoucherCodes.ae, DCMnetwork and more, so we hold a firm line: we do not take on business that competes with us or you. Your work, your data and your edge stay yours.

What does it cost? A 30-day trial, by the job or by the hour. Only pay if you’re happy.

Start with a 30-day trial, up to 50 hours per role. If you’re happy and want to keep going, you pay for the work from the start, by the job or by the hour, whichever works better for you. If it isn’t right, walk away and pay nothing. You only ever pay when you’re happy with the work.

By the job: a set price for each task, like an order processed, a ticket resolved or an invoice handled, so you pay for the result and never for our hours or how we split the work between people and AI. By the hour: for variable or judgement-heavy work, you only pay for the time worked. We’ll recommend whichever works better for you.

Either way: no fixed contract, no lock-in, no hidden fees and no surprise invoices.
