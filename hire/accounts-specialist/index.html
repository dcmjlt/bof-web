Frequently asked questions

What growing teams ask us first.

Will you work with a business that competes with mine? No. We run our own digital businesses, so we never take on anyone who competes with you.

No. We run our own digital businesses on this exact team, TicketsToDo, VoucherCodes.ae, DCMnetwork and more, so we hold a firm line: we do not take on business that competes with us or you. Your work, your data and your edge stay yours.

Do you replace our accountant or finance lead? No. We run the operational accounts work; advice, sign-off and strategy stay with you.

No. We run the operational accounts work, payables, receivables, invoicing, credit control and reconciliation. Financial advice, sign-off and strategy stay with your accountant or finance lead; we keep the day-to-day clean for them.

What does it cost? A 30-day trial, by the job or by the hour. Only pay if you’re happy.

There’s no catch, and it isn’t free work. Try as many roles as you need, up to 50 hours per role, over your first 30 days. If you’re happy and want to keep going, you pay for the work from the start, by the job or by the hour, whichever works better for you. If it isn’t right, walk away and pay nothing. You only ever pay when you’re happy with the work.

By the job: a set price for each task, like an invoice raised or a payment processed, so you pay for the result and never for our hours or how we split the work between people and AI.
