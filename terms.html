The trial

You can try the work over a 30-day trial window. You may try as many roles as you need, up to 50 hours per role over the trial, with no obligation. If you choose to continue because you’re happy with the work, the work is chargeable from the start, including the trial work, by the job or by the hour, whichever works better for you. If you do not continue, nothing is payable. So you only ever pay if you’re happy with the work. No fixed term, no lock-in, and you can stop anytime.

We agree the specific scope of any trial with you on a short kickoff call before it starts. If you continue after the trial, we put a short written service agreement in place covering the work and how we charge. There is no fixed term and no minimum spend, and you can stop anytime.

To keep things clear: by the hour means an hour of an operator’s or AI agent’s worked time on your tasks; by the job means a set price per completed unit of work, for example an order processed, a ticket resolved or an invoice entered. The trial is for genuinely evaluating the work, so we may pause it if use goes well beyond a reasonable evaluation of a role.
