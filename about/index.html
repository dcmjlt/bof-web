From the founder

Why we opened this up.

I have spent years running digital and ecommerce businesses, TicketsToDo, VoucherCodes.ae, DCMnetwork and more. The thing that quietly decides whether a growing business stays sane is the back office: the bookkeeping, the support, the order and document processing, the reconciliation, the admin that never stops.

We got good at it because we had to. For our own businesses. We built a team of trained operators and AI agents on one workflow. People for the judgement work. Agents for the repetitive volume. The same operation that holds our own products to a high bar.

Back Office Factory is that operation, opened up to other founders. We do not take on business that competes with us or you, so your work and your data stay yours. And we price it the way I would want to be priced: by the job or by the hour, whichever works better for you, a straight number early, no lock-in, no surprises.

Rahman Hussain Founder, Back Office Factory

The proof

We run our own digital businesses on this exact team.

The strongest thing we can say about our back office is that we trust it with our own.

  • TicketsToDo, an events ticketing marketplace. Order volumes through peak season are exactly the load we test the support workflow against.
  • VoucherCodes.ae, the UAE deals platform. The reconciliation and partner-coordination work is what taught us to build agents into the workflow.
  • DCMnetwork, our digital media network. Where document processing at scale stops being an admin problem and becomes a margin problem.
  • And more.

How we work

A few things we will not move on.

Honesty

Honest, simple pricing

By the job or by the hour, whichever works better for you. No lock-in, no hidden fees and no surprise invoices, ever. A clear, honest rate once we understand the work.

Independence

We protect your edge

We do not take on business that competes with us or you. Your data, your customers and your operational edge stay yours.

Discretion

We stay behind your brand

Your customers see your brand and your suppliers see your company. We run the back office behind the scenes, so your customers and suppliers deal with you, not us. You own the relationship and you keep the credit.

Delivery

People plus AI, one workflow

People for the judgement, agents for the volume, on one workflow we run end to end. You never have to operate, learn or babysit the AI. That’s our job, not yours.

Scope

We tell you straight

We deliver the work, operations, build, design and support, rather than sell advice. If something sits outside that, we say so plainly rather than stretch and underdeliver.

Reversibility

No lock-in

No fixed contract and no lock-in. Start with a 30-day trial and stay because the work is good, not because you’re tied in. If it isn’t right, walk away and pay nothing.
