From the founder

Why we opened this up.

Founder photo
Placeholder · real photo of Rahman Hussain required before launch

I have spent years running digital and ecommerce businesses, TicketsToDo, VoucherCodes.ae, DCMnetwork and more. The thing that quietly decides whether a growing business stays sane is the back office: the bookkeeping, the support, the order and document processing, the reconciliation, the admin that never stops.

We got good at it because we had to. For our own businesses. We built a team — trained operators, AI agents — on one workflow. People for the judgement work. Agents for the repetitive volume. The same operation that holds our own products to a high bar.

Back Office Factory is that operation, opened up to other founders. We do not take on business that competes with us or you, so your work and your data stay yours. And we price it the way I would want to be priced: by the hour, a straight number early, no lock-in, no surprises.

Rahman Hussain Founder, Back Office Factory
