What we collect when you ask for an estimate, why, and what we do with it.

This policy covers the information you give us through the “Get your estimate” form on this website. We keep it short and plain on purpose. We do not run trackers, advertising pixels or marketing lists on this site.

Who we are

Back Office Factory (Placeholder · confirm registered legal entity name, company number and registered address) is the data controller for the information you submit here.

What we collect

When you submit the estimate form, we collect only what you enter:

  • Your name
  • Your business email address
  • Your business website
  • An optional description of the work that needs running

We also record which page the form was submitted from, so we know how you reached us. We do not ask for, or want, sensitive personal information in the free-text field — please don’t include it.

Why we use it

We use this information for one purpose: to prepare your estimate and reply to you about it. We do not add you to a mailing list, we do not sell or share your details for marketing, and we do not send unrelated email. The lawful basis is our legitimate interest in responding to a request you have made of us.

How it is handled

When you submit the form, our website host (Cloudflare) runs a short piece of code we wrote that receives the submission, applies a simple anti-spam check, and forwards your details directly to a private notification channel only the Back Office Factory team can see (Slack). We do not store the submission anywhere else; it lives only in that Slack channel so we can reply. Both Cloudflare and Slack process the data on our instructions as part of delivering the website.
